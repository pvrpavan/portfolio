Fake Product Detection Through BlockChain

Counterfeit products undermine consumer trust, harm manufacturer revenue, and disrupt market integrity. Traditional methods are often inefficient, prone to tampering. To address this, we propose a Blockchain-based product. Our system securely stores product data on a decentralized blockchain ledger and assigns a unique barcode or QR code to each product. Manufacturers register and manage product details. Consumers can scan these codes via a mobile or web application to instantly verify authenticity.

Key Features:

  • Blockchain-Powered Security
  • Unique QR/Barcode for Each Product
  • Instant Consumer Verification
  • Decentralized & Transparent System
  • Real-Time Product Authentication
